Automakers and workers face existential fight over robots, future

Source: Hartford Courant  ·  June 20, 2026

Automakers and workers are in a high-stakes battle over automation, with collaborative robots (cobots) already deployed at GM's Factory ZERO in Detroit. The conflict took center stage at the UAW Constitutional Convention and a major industry conference focused on AI, robotics, and manufacturing.

The Atomic take

The deployment of cobots at GM's Factory ZERO turns an abstract automation debate into a concrete bargaining issue, which is exactly why it surfaced at the UAW Constitutional Convention rather than staying confined to an industry conference. Watch whether the union pushes for contract language governing how collaborative robots are introduced on the line and how job classifications shift around them. The outcome here will set expectations for the next round of Detroit contract talks across all three automakers.
