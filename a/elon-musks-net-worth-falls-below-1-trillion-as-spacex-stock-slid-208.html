Elon Musk's Net Worth Falls Below $1 Trillion As SpaceX Stock Slides

Source: Business Insider  ·  June 23, 2026

A global tech stock sell-off has pushed Elon Musk's net worth below the $1 trillion mark, dropping him to approximately $957 billion according to Bloomberg's Billionaires Index. The decline was driven by falling share prices in both Tesla and SpaceX following the company's post-IPO correction. Musk had briefly become the world's first trillionaire after SpaceX's public debut.

The Atomic take

SpaceX's valuation now moves Musk's fortune in real time, which is new: a privately held rocket company that was long shielded from public markets is suddenly exposed to the same sell-offs hitting Tesla. The post-IPO correction matters less for Musk's ranking than for what it reveals about how investors price Starship and Starlink once they can actually trade the stock. Watch whether SpaceX's share slide forces tighter disclosure on launch cadence and Starlink subscriber growth, the metrics that will now set its floor.
