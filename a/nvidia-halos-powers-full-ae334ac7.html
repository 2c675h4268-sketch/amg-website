NVIDIA Halos powers full

Source: Interesting Engineering  ·  June 22, 2026

NVIDIA introduced Halos for Robotics, a new safety platform designed to help developers build and deploy autonomous robots that can operate alongside people in factories, warehouses, and logistics centers. The system combines compute, software, and safety tools for humanoid and autonomous machines.

The Atomic take

Safety certification has been the quiet bottleneck keeping humanoids penned behind cages, so NVIDIA bundling compute, software, and safety validation into one platform directly targets what regulators and insurers actually demand before robots share floor space with workers. The real test is whether Halos meets recognized functional-safety standards like ISO 13849 or the emerging ISO 10218 rules for collaborative machines, not just NVIDIA's internal benchmarks. Watch which warehouse and logistics operators commit to pilots, and whether competitors building on other compute stacks get locked out of these deployments.
