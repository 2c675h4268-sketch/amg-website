range strikes on Russia hit a major natural gas plant and satellite centers

Source: Breitbart News Network  ·  June 23, 2026

Ukrainian forces have conducted long-range strikes targeting a major natural gas processing plant and two key satellite communications centers inside Russia. The satellite facilities represent a significant escalation in the targeting of Russian space and communications infrastructure. The strikes mark a notable development in the ongoing conflict's intersection with critical energy and satellite networks.

The Atomic take

Hitting satellite communications centers, not just energy infrastructure, pushes the war into Russia's ground-based space command links, the nodes that relay targeting data, troop coordination, and secure military traffic. If these strikes degrade uplink and downlink capacity, Russian forces could lose real-time connectivity for drones and precision weapons in contested zones. Watch whether Moscow reroutes through redundant or commercial satellite assets and whether Kyiv treats these facilities as repeat targets rather than one-off hits.
