1 of the Most Interesting AI Stocks in the Market Isn't a Tech Company

Source: Barchart  ·  June 23, 2026

Recursion Pharmaceuticals is emerging as a standout AI-driven story within the biotech sector, using artificial intelligence to reimagine how drugs are discovered and developed. The company's approach positions it as a potential major winner in the AI investment landscape beyond traditional tech firms. Analysts are watching Recursion closely as it attempts to prove that biotech can be the next frontier for AI-driven returns.

The Atomic take

Recursion's pitch matters because it tests whether AI can compress the slow, expensive grind of drug discovery into something faster and cheaper, which is the real bottleneck for longevity therapeutics. The question analysts should press isn't the AI hype but the clinical readouts: does Recursion have molecules advancing through trials with results that justify the platform's promise? Watch its pipeline milestones and partnership revenue over the next few quarters, since those are what separate a working drug engine from an interesting demo.
