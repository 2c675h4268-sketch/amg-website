'A better way to be relevant': Greens embrace data center fight

Source: POLITICO  ·  July 19, 2026

Major environmental groups including the Sierra Club and Natural Resources Defense Council have joined opposition to data center development and are beginning to notch victories. The effort provides environmental organizations a new rallying cause after setbacks over the past 18 months.

The Atomic take

Green groups pivoting to data center opposition means the compute buildout now faces organized legal and permitting resistance from the same coalitions that slowed pipelines and coal plants, not just local NIMBY holdouts. That matters because hyperscalers are racing to site AI capacity fast, and Sierra Club or NRDC challenges over water use, grid strain, and emissions can add years and cost to projects. Watch which specific data center approvals these groups target next, and whether utilities respond by fast-tracking the very gas and nuclear generation that could deepen the fight.
