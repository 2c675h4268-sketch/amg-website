Australia's Firmus Technologies strikes AI access deal with Nvidia

Source: Reuters  ·  June 28, 2026

Australian AI infrastructure company Firmus Technologies signed a strategic partnership with Nvidia to provide emerging AI firms with more cost-effective access to computing power.

The Atomic take

Securing preferential access to Nvidia hardware is the hard part of building an AI business right now, so a smaller player like Firmus locking in supply matters more than the partnership language suggests, especially for startups that cannot outbid hyperscalers for GPU allocation. The real test is whether the promised cost advantage survives contact with Australia's power prices and data center buildout timelines. Watch for concrete capacity figures and which emerging firms actually land allocations, since access deals often look generous until the chips ship.
