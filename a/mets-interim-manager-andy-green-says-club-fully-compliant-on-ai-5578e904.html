Mets interim manager Andy Green says club fully compliant on AI following report of their usage

Source: The Associated Press  ·  July 18, 2026

New York Mets interim manager Andy Green responded to a report about the organization using AI for in-game strategy decisions, stating the club is fully compliant with Major League Baseball regulations.

The Atomic take

When a professional sports team has to publicly affirm it follows the rulebook on AI, it means leagues are already drawing lines around where machine assistance ends and human judgment must begin during live competition. MLB has restrictions on in-game technology use, so the real question is what counts as "compliant" when clubs feed data into models between pitches versus during them. Watch whether the league clarifies its AI policy or opens a formal review, since ambiguity here invites every other franchise to test the same boundary.
