About

One connected view of where the world is going.

Atomic Media Group is an independent publication covering the five forces reshaping the next decade: artificial intelligence, robotics, space, longevity, and the energy and compute powering them.

Why Atomic exists

The technologies shaping the future are usually covered in isolation. One outlet follows AI. Another follows space, or longevity, or energy. But these forces do not move independently, they compound. AI drives demand for compute and power. Robotics depends on advances in AI. The economics of longevity and space are being rewritten by both.

Atomic Media Group was created to connect them, explain their implications, and give readers a clearer picture of where the world is going, without having to follow hundreds of sources.

Our editorial approach

We cover areas filled with hype and speculation. Our job is to cut through it. Every week, Atomic:

  • Prioritizes primary and reputable sources. We start from original reporting, filings, and research, not second-hand summaries.
  • Distinguishes reported facts from analysis. What happened and what it might mean are kept clearly separate.
  • Links to original reporting. Every development points back to its source so you can verify and read further.
  • Corrects meaningful errors. When we get something wrong, we fix it and say so.
  • Avoids presenting speculation as established fact. Forecasts are labeled as forecasts.

Independent, and transparent about it

Atomic Media Group is an independent publication. Our editorial judgment is our own. We are transparent about the partners who help make the brief free for every reader.
